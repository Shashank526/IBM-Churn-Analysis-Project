#IBM Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. Losing existing customers directly impacts revenue, profitability, and long-term growth.

This project analyzes customer churn patterns using the Telco Customer Churn dataset and transforms raw customer data into actionable business insights through data analysis, SQL querying, and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

The primary objective is to identify the factors driving customer attrition, quantify revenue risk, and provide strategic recommendations that help businesses improve customer retention.

---

## Business Problem

Customer acquisition costs are significantly higher than customer retention costs. Organizations need to understand:

* Why customers leave
* Which customer segments are at the highest risk
* How churn impacts revenue
* What actions can reduce customer attrition

This project addresses these questions through data-driven analysis and visualization.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze customer churn behavior and trends
* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Measure revenue impact caused by churn
* Build an executive-level Power BI dashboard
* Generate actionable business recommendations
* Support data-driven retention strategies

## Key Business Insights

### 1. Month-to-Month Customers Have Highest Churn

Customers without long-term commitments are significantly more likely to leave.

### 2. Fiber Optic Customers Show Elevated Churn

Despite premium offerings, fiber optic users demonstrate relatively high churn behavior.

### 3. Electronic Check Users Are More Likely to Churn

Customers using electronic checks exhibit higher attrition rates than auto-payment users.

### 4. New Customers Are Most Vulnerable

The highest churn risk occurs during the early stages of the customer lifecycle.

### 5. Service Adoption Influences Retention

Customers with different service combinations display distinct churn patterns that can guide targeted retention campaigns.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Improve Contract Retention

Encourage migration from month-to-month contracts to annual plans through incentives and discounts.

### Strengthen Customer Onboarding

Implement engagement programs for newly acquired customers to improve early-stage retention.

### Promote Automatic Payments

Offer benefits for customers using auto-payment methods.

### Enhance Customer Support

Focus customer success efforts on high-risk segments.

### Monitor Service Quality

Regularly evaluate customer satisfaction among fiber optic subscribers.

### Launch Proactive Retention Campaigns

Use churn indicators to intervene before customers leave.
